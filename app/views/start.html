UK Emissions Trading Registry

The UK Emissions Trading Registy is a tool to manage Allowances for emissions of greenhouse gases as part of the UK's climate change policy.

Before you start

You'll need a user account set up before you can use this system. Ask your systems administrator to invite you to register, and set up an account here when you receive the invitation.

Registering your organisation

If your organisation is not yet registered as a participant in the UK Emissions Trading Scheme, an authorised representative of the organisation must register the organisation first.
