Decision Notice

  1. The appeal is allowed.
  2. The decision made by the Secretary of State on 13/06/2017 in respect of the PIP is set aside.
  3. Warren Sutton is entitled to the daily living component at the standard rate from 12/07/2017 to 11/07/2020.
  4. Warren Sutton has limited ability to carry out activities of daily living. he scores 8 points. He satisfies the following descriptors:
